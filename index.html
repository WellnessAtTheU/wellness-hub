KPI Framework
Division-Level Key Performance Indicators FY2025 — 4 KPIs
WELL-BEING SELF-PERCEPTION
88%
Target: 89% · Base: 88%
What it measures: % of students who rate their overall health as "good" or better.
Data source: NCHA Survey
Question: "How would you describe your overall health?"
Linked to: Health Literacy Canvas Course · Coaching/Peer Navigation · Preventive Outreach
CAMPUS CLIMATE FOR WELL-BEING
42%
Target: 43% · Base: 42%
What it measures: % of students who agree the U prioritizes health and supports open dialogue about wellness.
Data source: NCHA Survey
Questions: "I feel students' health is a priority at the U" · "The U encourages open discussion about health"
Linked to: Health Communications Pilot · Branding & Social Norming · Advisory Board
CULTURE OF CARE SCORE
44%
Target: 45% · Base: 44%
What it measures: % of students who agree campus provides mutual support and care for one another.
Data source: NCHA Survey
Question: "We are a campus where we look out for each other."
Linked to: Peer Well-Being Navigators · Spiritual Wellness · Student Advisory Board
SUPPORT SERVICES UTILIZATION
68%
Target: Growing · 71K+ interactions
What it measures: # of students (unique and total) utilizing services in the SH&W reporting line.
Data source: Campus Connect · Individual Office Utilization Tracking
Targets: Utilization within dept · All students · Unique students · Programming events
Linked to: Unified EHR (Medicat) · Referral Pathway Development · Utilization Dashboard
